📌 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
Project Title:
Placement Cell Management System (PCMS)

Introduction:
The Placement Cell Management System is a web-based application designed to automate and streamline campus recruitment activities. It acts as a bridge between students, placement officers, and companies. The system manages student records, company details, job postings, eligibility filtering, application tracking, interview scheduling, and placement statistics.

The chatbot integrated into this system will act as a 24/7 virtual placement assistant answering student queries related to placements, eligibility, deadlines, companies, training sessions, resumes, interviews, and policies.

🎯 2. OBJECTIVES OF THE PROJECT
To automate the placement process.

To maintain centralized student and company database.

To reduce paperwork.

To provide real-time placement updates.

To allow students to apply online.

To track placement statistics.

To provide chatbot-based instant query resolution.

💬 6. COMPLETE CHATBOT TRAINING CONTENT (Q&A FORMAT)
Below is ready-made structured Q&A data.

🔹 A. REGISTRATION & LOGIN QUERIES
Q1: How can I register for placements?
A: You need to login to the Placement Cell portal and complete your profile including academic details, resume upload, and contact information. After profile approval by the Placement Officer, you will become eligible to apply.

Q2: I forgot my password. What should I do?
A: Click on the "Forgot Password" option on the login page and enter your registered email ID to reset your password.

Q3: Why is my profile not approved yet?
A: Profile approval may take 24–48 hours. Ensure all mandatory fields and resume are uploaded correctly.

🔹 B. ELIGIBILITY RELATED QUERIES
Q4: Am I eligible for a company?
A: Eligibility depends on:

Minimum CGPA requirement

Allowed branches

Backlog criteria

Year of study

Check the company details section for eligibility information.

Q5: I have one backlog. Can I apply?
A: It depends on company policy. Some companies allow no active backlogs, while others allow limited backlogs.

🔹 C. COMPANY & JOB DETAILS
Q6: How can I check company details?
A: Go to the "Company Listings" section to view:

Job role

Package

Eligibility

Deadline

Selection process

Q7: What is the highest package offered?
A: The highest package varies yearly. Please check the Placement Statistics section for updated data.

🔹 D. APPLICATION STATUS
Q8: How do I know if I am shortlisted?
A: Check your dashboard under "Application Status". You will also receive an email notification.

Q9: Can I withdraw my application?
A: Application withdrawal depends on placement policy. Contact the Placement Officer.

🔹 E. INTERVIEW RELATED
Q10: Where can I see interview schedule?
A: Interview details are available under the "Interview Schedule" section in your dashboard.

Q11: What should I carry for the interview?
A: Carry:

Multiple resume copies

College ID card

Passport size photos

Academic documents

🔹 F. RESUME GUIDELINES
Q12: What format should my resume be in?
A: Resume should be in PDF format, maximum 2 pages, professional template, and no spelling errors.

Q13: Can I update my resume after applying?
A: Resume updates may not reflect for already applied companies.

🔹 G. TRAINING & INTERNSHIP
Q14: Are internships included in placements?
A: Yes, internship opportunities are posted separately under the Internship section.

Q15: Does the college provide training before placements?
A: Yes, the Placement Cell organizes aptitude tests, technical training, mock interviews, and soft skill sessions.

🔹 H. PLACEMENT POLICY QUESTIONS
Q16: What is the one student one job policy?
A: Once a student gets placed in a company with a package above a specified threshold, they may not be allowed to sit for further companies.

Q17: Can I apply for multiple companies?
A: Yes, until you are placed, subject to policy guidelines.

🔹 I. RESULT & REJECTION
Q18: Why was I rejected?
A: Rejection may be based on:

Technical round performance

HR interview

Communication skills

Aptitude test

Q19: Can I reapply to the same company?
A: Generally no, unless the company reopens applications.

🔹 J. STATISTICS RELATED
Q20: What is the placement percentage?
A: Placement percentage = (Number of students placed / Total eligible students) × 100
